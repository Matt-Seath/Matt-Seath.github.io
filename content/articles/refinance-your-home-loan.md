If your current loan hasn't been reviewed in the last 12 to 24 months, there's a high probability you're overpaying.

At Auri Finance, we help you refinance strategically. That means lower repayments, better structure, and access to equity if needed.

## Refinance with costs covered (for eligible clients)

In many cases, we can help you refinance with little to no out of pocket cost.

Depending on the lender and your scenario, benefits may include:

- Cashback offers from lenders
- Fee waivers
- Credits to offset discharge and application costs

This means you can switch loans without being financially set back upfront.

*Eligibility and lender policies apply. We'll confirm this upfront before proceeding.*

## What you can achieve by refinancing

### Lower your monthly repayments
Move to a more competitive rate and improve cashflow immediately.

### Access equity without selling
Use your property to fund an investment, renovation, or consolidate debt.

### Consolidate high interest debts
Credit cards and personal loans rolled into one lower rate facility.

### Reset your loan structure
Switch to interest only, fix part of your loan, or restructure for future purchases.

## Who this is for

- Your rate is above market
- Your fixed rate is expiring soon
- You want to pull out equity
- Your financial situation has improved
- You haven't reviewed your loan in years

If any of the above applies, refinancing is a missed opportunity if you don't act.

## What most borrowers don't realise

Banks rarely reward loyalty. In many cases, new customers receive better rates than existing ones.

If you don't actively review your loan, you are likely paying a premium.

## Costs to consider

- Discharge fee from your current lender
- Government registration fees
- Potential break costs if your loan is fixed

Where possible, we structure your refinance so these are minimised or offset.
