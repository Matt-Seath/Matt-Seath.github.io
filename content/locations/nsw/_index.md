## NSW property market

New South Wales remains the largest and most active property market in Australia. Sydney consistently leads the country in property values, with strong demand across the inner suburbs, lower north shore, and western corridors. Despite periods of correction, Sydney's long term growth trajectory has rewarded patient buyers and investors.

Outside Sydney, areas like the Hunter Valley, Illawarra, and the Central Coast have attracted growing numbers of buyers seeking more space and affordability while maintaining proximity to the city. Regional NSW has also seen increased interest from remote workers who no longer need to commute daily.

First home buyers in NSW can access stamp duty concessions for properties under certain thresholds, and the First Home Guarantee scheme allows eligible buyers to purchase with as little as 5% deposit without paying lenders mortgage insurance.

Investor activity in NSW remains significant, particularly in suburbs with strong rental yields and proximity to infrastructure. We help investors assess loan products that align with their strategy, whether that is interest only repayments, offset accounts, or fixed rate periods.
