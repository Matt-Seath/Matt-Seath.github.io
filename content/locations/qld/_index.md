## Queensland first home buyer grants and concessions

Queensland offers one of the most generous first home buyer packages in Australia. Here is what you may be eligible for:

**First Home Owner Grant (FHOG):** $30,000 for new homes valued under $750,000. This applies to newly built homes, units, and townhouses. It does not apply to established properties.

**Stamp duty concession:** First home buyers pay no transfer duty on homes valued up to $550,000. A sliding concession applies for homes valued between $550,000 and $700,000. For a $600,000 home, this could save you around $8,750 compared to the standard rate.

**First Home Guarantee:** Eligible buyers can purchase with as little as 5% deposit without paying Lenders Mortgage Insurance (LMI). On a $600,000 purchase, avoiding LMI could save you $15,000 to $25,000.

**Example:** A first home buyer purchasing a new $650,000 townhouse in South Brisbane could receive the $30,000 FHOG, save on stamp duty through the concession, and avoid LMI through the First Home Guarantee. That is potentially over $50,000 in savings before you even move in.

We help you identify which grants and schemes you qualify for and factor them into your loan strategy from day one.

## Frequently asked questions

**How much deposit do I need to buy in Queensland?**
Most lenders require a minimum of 5% deposit. With less than 20%, you will typically pay Lenders Mortgage Insurance unless you qualify for the First Home Guarantee or a similar government scheme. On a $700,000 property, a 5% deposit is $35,000 and a 20% deposit is $140,000.

**What are the costs of buying a property in QLD?**
Beyond your deposit, budget for stamp duty (unless you qualify for a first home buyer concession), conveyancing fees ($1,500 to $2,500), building and pest inspections ($400 to $800), and loan establishment fees (varies by lender, often waived).

**Can I buy a property in QLD if I live interstate?**
Yes. Many of our clients purchase QLD investment properties while living in other states. The process is the same, and we handle everything digitally.

**How long does the home loan process take?**
From initial consultation to unconditional approval typically takes 2 to 4 weeks, depending on the lender and the complexity of your application. Settlement usually occurs 4 to 6 weeks after contracts are signed.
