## Your Local Mortgage Broker in Calamvale

Calamvale is a thriving suburb in Brisbane's southern corridor, located about 19 kilometres from the CBD. Over the past two decades it has transformed from a largely undeveloped area into one of the south side's most popular residential addresses. The suburb combines modern housing, quality schools, parklands, and convenient shopping at Calamvale Central. Its position along major transport routes, including bus services and easy freeway access, makes commuting practical for residents who work across Brisbane. Calamvale's continued growth and strong community infrastructure make it an excellent place to buy or invest.

## Property Landscape in Calamvale

Calamvale features a newer housing stock compared to many surrounding suburbs. The majority of homes were built from the late 1990s onward, giving buyers access to modern designs with contemporary layouts and finishes. You will find a healthy mix of detached houses, townhouses, and apartment complexes. Detached homes on blocks of 400 to 700 square metres are common, while townhouse developments cater to buyers seeking something more compact and low maintenance. The suburb also has a number of gated communities and master planned pockets that offer additional amenities. This variety of property types creates options at multiple price points, from entry level units through to spacious executive homes.

## Who Buys in Calamvale?

Calamvale attracts a notably diverse buyer profile. Young families form a large part of the market, drawn by the modern homes, well regarded schools such as Calamvale Community College and Stretton State College, and the abundance of parks and playgrounds throughout the suburb. First home buyers find the townhouse and unit segment provides an achievable starting point, particularly with government grants and incentives that may apply to newer properties. Investors are well represented in the suburb, attracted by strong tenant demand and the suburb's ongoing population growth. Professionals who commute south to Logan or north to the CBD appreciate Calamvale's central position along the Pacific Motorway corridor.
