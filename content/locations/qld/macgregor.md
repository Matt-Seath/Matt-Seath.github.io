## Your Local Mortgage Broker in Macgregor

Macgregor is a mature, well regarded suburb in Brisbane's south, situated roughly 14 kilometres from the city centre. It benefits from a prime position between the major retail and dining precinct of Sunnybank and the green spaces of Toohey Forest. Residents enjoy a suburban lifestyle with excellent accessibility, including bus services, proximity to the South East Freeway, and easy connections to surrounding employment hubs. Macgregor's blend of convenience, natural surroundings, and community character makes it a consistently popular choice for home buyers and investors alike.

## Property Landscape in Macgregor

Macgregor's streetscape is defined by a mix of established family homes, many of which were built during the suburb's initial development in the 1970s and 1980s. These properties sit on relatively large lots, offering space for families and, in many cases, scope for renovation or future subdivision. In recent years, modern townhouse developments have added variety to the market, providing more affordable and low maintenance alternatives. A small number of apartment complexes round out the options. The diversity of housing stock means that Macgregor caters to a broad range of budgets, from entry level townhouses through to substantial family homes on generous blocks.

## Who Buys in Macgregor?

The suburb attracts a wide range of buyers. Families are a core group, valuing the quiet streets, nearby schools including Macgregor State School and Macgregor State High School, and the abundance of parks and green corridors linked to Toohey Forest. First home buyers find the townhouse market provides a realistic entry point in a suburb with strong long term fundamentals. Investors benefit from solid rental demand, driven in part by the suburb's proximity to Griffith University at Nathan. Professionals who work in the southern business districts or commute to the CBD also make up a notable portion of the buyer pool, attracted by Macgregor's practical location and relatively short travel times.
