## Your Local Mortgage Broker in Runcorn

Runcorn is a popular residential suburb in Brisbane's south, approximately 16 kilometres from the CBD. It has earned a strong reputation as a practical, affordable, and convenient place to call home. With excellent public transport connections, easy freeway access, and a selection of parks and green spaces, Runcorn appeals to a wide range of buyers who want suburban comfort without sacrificing accessibility.

## Property Landscape in Runcorn

The suburb is characterised by a mix of detached houses, duplexes, and townhouse complexes. Many of the original homes date from the 1970s and 1980s, built on blocks that are generous by today's standards. These properties often present opportunities for renovation or redevelopment, attracting both owner-occupiers and investors. Newer townhouse and duplex developments have added more compact, low maintenance options to the market. Overall, Runcorn tends to offer more affordable price points than neighbouring suburbs like Sunnybank, making it an attractive proposition for buyers entering the market or looking to maximise rental yield.

## Who Buys in Runcorn?

Runcorn draws a diverse buyer pool. First home buyers find the suburb particularly appealing because of its relatively lower entry prices and proximity to employment hubs along the south side corridor. Young families value the access to schools such as Runcorn State School and Runcorn State High School, along with the abundance of parks and playgrounds. Investors are active in the market here, drawn by strong rental demand and yields that compare favourably to many other Brisbane suburbs. The suburb's proximity to Griffith University's Nathan campus also generates demand from students and academic staff seeking nearby rental accommodation.
