## SA property market

Adelaide has long been considered one of Australia's most undervalued capital cities, and in recent years the market has begun to correct that perception. Strong price growth across Adelaide's inner and middle ring suburbs has attracted attention from both local and interstate buyers.

Suburbs like Prospect, Norwood, Unley, and Salisbury have seen consistent demand. The northern and southern corridors continue to offer value for first home buyers, while areas close to the city and the coast appeal to upgraders and investors.

South Australia's first home buyer incentives include stamp duty relief for eligible purchases and the First Home Owner Grant for new builds. These schemes, combined with Adelaide's relatively lower entry prices, make SA one of the more accessible markets for people entering the property ladder.

Regional South Australia also presents opportunities, particularly in towns with strong local economies or tourism appeal. Whether you are looking at Mount Gambier, Murray Bridge, or the Barossa, we can help you find a lender comfortable with regional lending.
